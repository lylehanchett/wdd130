Some of our most popular tours

Any age Mild rapids trip

This trip is perfect for any age and all beginers. It has many lower class rapids with calm water before and after. It is fun and safe with beautiful scenery. Come start your adventure and learn how to raft the rapids with us.

Scenery Tour

This tour is mostly about the scenery. It has access to areas not accessible by land. It has some mild rapids with some fast moving water, but mostly its slower water that can be enjoyes while relaxing and taking in the views.

Extreme Rapids

This trip is for those with prior experience in rafting. Must be 18 years of age and a profecient swimmer. While our guides are experienced and we do pretraining before these trips its always better to be prepared for mother natures extreme power.
